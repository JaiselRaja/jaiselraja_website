Why These Notes?

During my Class 12 journey, I faced significant challenges in truly understanding concepts and deriving expressions step by step. Many times, the explanations in the Tamil Nadu State Board textbooks—especially in Physics—skipped important intermediate steps and lacked clear, step-by-step explanations for Example Problems, making it difficult to build a clear and logical understanding.

To overcome this, I created my own notes that focus on complete and structured derivations, ensuring that no crucial step is missed. I have also emphasized visual representations of concepts, which make complex ideas easier to understand and remember. These notes also provide clear and systematic approaches to problem solving, making it easier to understand and solve questions confidently.

The Chemistry notes include short notes of the concepts in the book, easy methods to study reactions in organic chemistry, and notes, problems, derivations, and visual representations in physical chemistry.

The Mathematics notes include solved problems along with the required formulas that need to be known before entering each set of problems.

I am releasing these notes to help students study derivations easily, understand concepts thoroughly with visual representation wherever necessary, approach problem solving with clarity, and save valuable time.
